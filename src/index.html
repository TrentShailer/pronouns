My pronouns are:

they/them/theirs/themselves

Examples:

  • I think they are very nice.
  • You talked to them yesterday.
  • Isn't their dog so cute!
  • That code is theirs.
  • They said they would do it themselves.

Singular "they" has been used in English to describe an unspecified person since the late 1300s.
